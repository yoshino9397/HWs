Becomes a Cat

Nyanko Sensei is a powerful beast youkai and commonly takes the form of a maneki-neko ("beckoning cat"). He acts as Natsume's bodyguard on the condition that when Natsume dies, he will inherit the Book of Friends, thus giving him control over a vast number of youkai.
